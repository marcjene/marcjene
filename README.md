## About me

I'm driven by the belief that sustainable energy solutions should work *with* people, not* around* them. My research sits at the intersection of **deep generative models** and **smart grid analytics**, building data-driven tools that bring visibility to what's happening behind the meter.

I'm completing a **PhD in Electrical Engineering** at [CITCEA-UPC](https://citcea.upc.edu/) (Barcelona), where my work focuses on probabilistic disaggregation of distributed energy resources — PV generation and heat pumps — from smart meter data, alongside non-technical loss detection in distribution grids. In parallel, I lead ML model development at **Tether EV**, and teach as a Substitute Professor at UPC, including a course co-designed around the *Moonshot Thinking* methodology.

I hold a BSc in Industrial Engineering and a double MSc in Energy from **KU Leuven** and **UPC**.

---

## 🔬 Research Focus

- **Probabilistic energy disaggregation** — reconstructing PV generation and heat pump consumption from aggregated smart meter readings using conditional denoising diffusion probabilistic models (cDDPM)
- **Grid-edge visibility** — data-driven approaches to improve observability at the low-voltage distribution level
- **Non-technical loss detection** — hybrid methods for fraud identification in distribution grids
- **Uncertainty quantification** — well-calibrated probabilistic forecasting for renewable energy systems
